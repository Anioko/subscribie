So, what happens next?

We'll process your order

Our network team will plan the connection from the exchange to your premesis, this can take between 1-3 days. Once the scoping has been completed you'll receive an email with your appointment date and contract information.


Sometimes you'll need to be home for your appointment, so we'll get in touch to work out what days are best for you. Engineer slots are either AM (8am till 1pm) or PM (Midday till 6pm).

We'll send out your modem

4-5 days before your line is due to go live, we'll send you a KC Modem in the post, this typically arrives 1-2 days before your line goes active.


We'll also email you our 'Welcome Guide' that explains how to get set up and online. We don't print this to save Mother Nature <3.

Your property will be connected

On your activation or installation date, an Openreach engineer will connect your property to Openreach network. This can happen anytime within your engineer appointment window. Depending on how complex your property or location is, this may be subject to delays but we'll let you know before this date.


PS: Your engineer probably won't be wearing a cape.

You're online!

You may need to wait till midnight of your activation date, but after the engineer has left you'll be good to go! Explore the web till your heart's content.


And if you're experiencing issues, our friendly UK support team are here to help!
