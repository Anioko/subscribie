  Do I have a Phone Line?

Internet can be delivered to a premises using a variety of methods, we here at KC Broadband use Openreach's telephone network. To see if you've already got a phone line, check the following:

  • If you currently receive broadband from TalkTalk, Sky, EE, PlusNet, BT you already have a phone line, so click 'Yes'

  • If you can find a telephone socket in your premises with an OpenReach or BT logo on it, click 'Yes'

  • If you're in a newly built house and have been informed by the developer that you don't have a phone line, click 'No'

  • If you are unsure, can't find any telephone sockets and your landlord/developer doesn't know, click 'No'
