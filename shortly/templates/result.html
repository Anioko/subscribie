About these results

We generated these results based on your line, we're upfront in that respect as there's nothing worse than signing up for a super fast package just to find you actually receive not even a tenth of the advertised speed. The speeds reported are based on OpenReach's network prediction, like all predictions they are not always 100% accurate. When processing your order we will again check your line to see if you're able to receive a good service and contact you if we think there may a problem (for example your line might not actually be able to receive Fibre).

The prices shown are generated assuming you have an in/active phone line installed in the premises, otherwise there may be an excess construction charge applied to your account - again we will discuss this with you before anything is actioned. The price also assumes you're paying by Direct Debit, our preferred payment method.
